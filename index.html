Welcome!

I am a PhD Candidate in Political Science at Columbia University

My research focuses on political behavior in the United States as citizens confront the prospect of democratic backsliding. I explore whether and how citizens respond to the extraordinary circumstances of contemporary American politics by engaging in unusual political behaviors. In my spare time I can be found wandering around Morningside Heights and playing racket sports with vastly varying degrees of skill

My research is or has been supported by the Center for Effective Lawmaking, the Columbia Experiment Labratory for the Social Sciences, the Institute for Humane Studies and the Stanford Polarization and Social Change Lab. I am indebted also to the collaboration of activist and political groups with whom I have cooperated on field experimental investigations of political behavior
